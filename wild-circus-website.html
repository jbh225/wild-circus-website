Our History

The wild circus was created in 2013 by Sacha Rabizov. He comes from a family of acrobats. Therefore since childhood, until now it has always been part of this world. Very strong in acrobatics, he always do his shows with alot of special effects. Its main purpose is to delight all audiences, from children to adults. Welcome to the world of the wild circus.
